Our Treatment Process

A structured, transparent path from your first visit to full recovery.

1. Consultation

Comprehensive assessment of your medical history, symptoms, and lifestyle to understand your needs.

2. Diagnosis

Advanced imaging and physical tests to pinpoint the exact cause of your shoulder pain.

3. Personalized Treatment

Development of a tailored treatment plan, ranging from manual therapy to surgical intervention if required.

4. Rehabilitation

Guided physiotherapy sessions focusing on restoring strength, flexibility, and function.

5. Recovery & Prevention

Final assessments and long-term strategies to maintain mobility and prevent future injuries.
